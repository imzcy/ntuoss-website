Introduction
The NTU Open Source Society (NTUOSS) is one of the pioneers in Nanyang Technological University in promoting the value and importance of Open Source to its students. As a committee, we focus on guiding the students of NTU in coping with the ever-changing Open Source community and also encourage students in contributing to various Open Source projects. Our motto is to educate as many students as possible in NTU and nurture a healthy environment for Open Source development in our University.
