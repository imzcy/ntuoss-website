Build and Break Technology
The Open Source Society (OSS) is a student community at the Nanyang Technological University helping to promte the value and importance of Open Source in NTU. We attract students of common technical interests to foster a community of engineers, hackers and builders to explore their interests in technology and share their knowledge and expertise with others.

Through our weekly meetings, we help students learn practical coding skills, find partners to work with on interesting projects and also help promote the value and importance of contributing to open source development.

We are NTU's resident hackers and we like to build things. That's why you should hack with us!
